Common Questions About Our Process

How long does the audit take?

You'll receive your report within 24 hours of submitting your information. The actual analysis takes our team about 2-3 hours per website.

Do I need to give you access to anything?

No, we can perform a comprehensive audit using only your website URL and the information you provide in the form. We don't need passwords or admin access.

What if my website is brand new?

That's perfect! It's much easier to fix problems before they start hurting your business. We can audit websites at any stage.

Can I implement the fixes myself?

Many of our recommendations can be implemented by business owners themselves. For more technical issues, we provide clear instructions that you can give to any web developer.

What if I don't understand something in the report?

We're always available to answer questions about your report. You can email us anytime for clarification.
