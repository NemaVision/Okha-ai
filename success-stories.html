Common Patterns in Success Stories

Looking at these results and dozens of other clients, we've noticed some consistent patterns:

Speed Issues Are Universal

Almost every small business website we audit is too slow. Fixing load times alone typically increases conversions by 40-60%.

Mobile Problems Cost the Most

With 60-80% of traffic coming from mobile devices, mobile usability problems have the biggest impact on lost customers.

Local SEO is Often Broken

Most local businesses aren't showing up in Google searches because of fixable technical issues, not competition.

Contact Methods Are Hidden

Businesses lose customers because phone numbers, addresses, and contact forms are hard to find or don't work properly.

Value Propositions Are Unclear

Visitors leave because they can't quickly understand what the business does or why they should choose them.

Trust Signals Are Missing

Websites without reviews, testimonials, or professional appearance lose credibility with potential customers.
