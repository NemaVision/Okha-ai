But business owners don't know how to fix these issues - and most web developers either don't explain the problems clearly or charge too much to fix them.

That's where we come in.

Our Story

I've been building websites for over 10 years. I've worked with everyone from Fortune 500 companies to local mom-and-pop shops. And here's what I learned:

Big companies have teams of people working on their websites. They have data analysts, user experience experts, and marketing specialists all working together to make sure their websites convert visitors into customers.

Small businesses don't have those resources. They usually get a website built once and then never think about it again. Meanwhile, that website could be losing them hundreds of customers every month.

That's not fair. And it's not necessary.

With the right knowledge and tools, any small business can have a website that competes with the big guys. That's what Okha.ai is all about.
